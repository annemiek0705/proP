Lost your charger? Battery’s dead but you want to make a video of your favorite artist’s performance? Worry not! We have a facility for borrowing items such as phone chargers(USB, Lightning, USB-C), external batteries and other items that you might need during your time at the festival, such as an umbrella or a raincoat!

You can find the borrow facilities’ locations on the maps inside the festival grounds.
